CHALLENGES

Challenges & Current Limitations

01

Cold Start Problem

ML models require large historical datasets; early-stage predictions may be unreliable without sufficient user data.

02

Data Privacy

Handling sensitive personal productivity data requires rigorous end-to-end encryption and secure authentication.

03

Platform Availability

Currently restricted to Android. Expansion to iOS and Web platforms is in the development roadmap.

04

Connectivity Dependency

While core features are offline-first, advanced cloud sync and backups require an active internet connection.

05

Prediction Accuracy

Early-stage ML accuracy is limited by the initial volume of behavioral data available for training.
